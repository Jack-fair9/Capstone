      How Does Our Simple Referal Process Work?

      Start An Account

      - Create an account for your company.
      - Have your employees create accounts.

      Start Looking

      - Create a job posting. Every week that the post stays up a percentage of the posting fee is alotted into an account, compounding into the referal reward. The longer the post is up, the higher the reward.
      - Employees reccomend you possible qualified candidates that they know.
      - The employer and candidate must acknowledge the referal and the candidate's relation to the referer before they can view the job post.

      Start Hiring

      - Let your candidates know that you want to continue to the interview process.
      - When the role is filled the reward is sent to the employee who recommended that candidate.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      While LinkedIn is a social networking site, Needel is simply a networking site. Our goal is to let employees connect their employers to possible job candidates and reward those repsonsible for the successful connections.

      Indeed is an aggregation site. It scrapes the internet for new job listings and clones the data into a new listing on it's site. Agregation sites do not, however, know when a listing has been filled, leaving the listing up for an indefinite amount of time. Every job posting on Needel is posted by an employer and the bounty cannot be collected until the listing is closed.

      When an employer posts a job they dictate whether it's an entry, mid, or senior level position. Once that's decided, they will decide how much of the posting fee will go towards the bounty (With no less than 25% going to administrative fees). Every week, the fee will compoud, meaning that the longer the job posting stays up, the more the bounty for filling it will be. If you reccomend a candidate that is hired for that role, you get the bounty.
